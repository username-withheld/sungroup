Company History

The Sun Group, Inc., is a South Florida based architectural firm providing a broad palette of architectural planning, design and consulting services. The Sun Group was established in 1991 by Florida Architect, Richard L Sun who serves as the firm's President and Principal Architect.  Richard brings his 28 years of architectural building experience to each of the firm's select projects.
 
In its 24 year history, The Sun Group has designed and built showcase buildings with its Client list of Florida's premiere property owners and developers.  The Sun Group has acquired an outstanding reputation for technical and design excellence.

In addition to its commercial clients, The Sun Group, Inc. has successfully served as the Architect and Consultant to numerous Local, State and Federal Government agencies with completed projects in Florida, South Carolina and Louisiana.
